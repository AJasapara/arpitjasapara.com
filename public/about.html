About Me

My name is Arpit Jasapara. I am originally from the Bay Area, but as of now I am a Computer Science student at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), class of 2020. Currently, I am a senior employee at the IT/Data Department in the UCLA Luskin School of Public Affairs. I am also an active member of several organizations on campus, including the Regents Scholar Society, Upsilon Pi Epsilon Honor Society, Association of Computing Machinery (ACM), and Bruin Entrepreneurs. My hobbies include practicing martial arts, especially Taekwondo, playing basketball, and freestyling performances.
