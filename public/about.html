About Me

Computer Science student at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA)

I am originally from the Bay Area, but now I am part of the UCLA Class of 2020. Currently, I am a senior employee at the IT/Data Department in the UCLA Luskin School of Public Affairs.

I am also an active member of several organizations on campus, including the Regents Scholar Society, Upsilon Pi Epsilon Honor Society, Association of Computing Machinery (ACM), and Bruin Entrepreneurs.

My hobbies include programming, practicing martial arts, especially Taekwondo, playing basketball, and freestyling performances.

My passion for Computer Science stems from the fact that this is the industry that is revolutionizing the world. The skills that I learn and the projects that I work on all contribute to that goal of making a positive difference.
